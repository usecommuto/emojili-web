Reporting objectionable content or abuse

You can report a puzzle, room, or user directly in the app (look for Report), and you can block a user from their profile or a room. You can also email us the details and we'll review and act — usually within 24 hours.

Deleting your account

In the app: You → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and data.

Common questions

  • Multiplayer won't join? Make sure the host created an online room (not "Pass & play") and is still on the lobby screen, and double-check the 5-letter code.
  • Lost your streak? Streaks advance once per day from the daily puzzle; email us if something looks wrong.
