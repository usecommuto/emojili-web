Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This policy explains what information the Emojili app ("Emojili", "we", "us") collects, why, and your choices. Questions? Email willis124578@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account details. If you sign in, we store your email address (via Sign in with Apple or a one-time email code) and a Supabase account identifier. Sign in with Apple may provide a private relay email; we never receive your Apple password.
  • Profile. A display name and avatar colour you choose, and an auto-generated friend code.
  • Gameplay. Your daily streak, puzzles solved, guess counts, friend connections, puzzles you create or send, and multiplayer room activity.
  • Push token. If you enable notifications, your device's push token so we can send the reminders you asked for.
  • Device basics. Standard technical data (app version, request logs) needed to run and secure the service.

You can also play as a guest, which keeps progress only on your device and creates no account.

How we use it

  • To run the game: save progress, sync your daily puzzle, deliver puzzles between friends, and host multiplayer rooms.
  • To send only the notifications you turn on. We never send advertising or promotional push.
  • To keep the service safe — enforce content rules, handle reports, and prevent abuse.
We do not track you. Emojili does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, does not collect your IDFA, and does not sell or share your data with data brokers.

Where your data lives

Account and gameplay data are stored with our backend provider, Supabase, protected by row-level security so you can only access your own data (and your friends' public profiles). Puzzle answers are stored as salted hashes so the app can grade guesses without exposing the answer.

Sharing

Content you choose to share is visible to its intended audience: a puzzle you send is visible to that friend; your display name, avatar and scores are visible to players in a multiplayer room you join. We do not otherwise share your personal information except as required to operate the service or comply with law.

Your choices & rights

  • Delete your account. In the app, go to You → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data from our servers.
  • Notifications. Turn reminders off any time in Settings → Notifications or in iOS Settings.
  • Access / correction. Email us to request a copy or correction of your data.

Children

Emojili is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.
